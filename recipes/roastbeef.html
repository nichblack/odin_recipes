Slow Cooker Roast Beef

A tender 10-hour roast beef that will leave your relatives wanting more next Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • Roast Beef
  • Worcestire Sauce
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Place the roast beef into the baking pan.
  2. Cover the roast beef in worcestire sauce
  3. Place into the oven at 450 degrees for 5 minutes.
  4. Finally, enjoy!
